Welcome to Page 2 of my website, this is the biography section! This is where you will you learn a bit more about me and my career as a journalism student and amateur photographer!

Journalism

I started my journalism career in 2017 and will be graduating hopefully in 2021. Throughout my degree I have learned about photojournalism, reporting, editing, coding and newspaper layout just to name a few. At the beginning of my journalism journey we were required to buy a DSLR camera, I bought mine from Best Buy Canada but there are many other great places to buy a DSLR. Below are a few suggestions listed if you are interested in buying one to take photos.

  • Best Buy Canada
  • Henry's Canada
  • Walmart Canada

Photography

A lot of inspiration for my photos comes from the calming effect of nature. I enjoy shooting (a term photographers use when they are taking photos) flowers, trees (especially willow trees) and old houses like Victorian-style homes.
